The NIST AI Resource Center (AIRC) was developed to support the operationalization of the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF). The AIRC offers access to relevant technical documents and resources (including software tools and guidance) to assist in the testing, evaluation, verification, and validation (TEVV) of AI.

Technical Reports that Support AI Risk Management

NIST research on AI standards, measurement, terminology, and more.

  • Securing Software Development Framework (SSDF) Version 1.2: Recommendations for Mitigating the Risk of Software Vulnerabilities NIST SP 800-218 REV. 1 (Initial Public Draft)

    Describes new and improved practices, tasks, and examples for the secure and reliable development, delivery, and improvement of software.

    • December 17th 2025
    • Draft

  • Assessing Risks and Impacts of AI (ARIA) 0.1: Pilot Evaluation Report NIST AI 700-2

    Describes the procedure used for a pilot of NIST’s Assessing Risks and Impacts of AI (ARIA) evaluation: ARIA 0.1. The pilot evaluation demonstrates the feasibility of a new approach to evaluation of AI systems: combining data from expert annotators and human testers, illustrated by a transparent measurement tool.

    • November, 2025

  • Global Engagement on AI Standards NIST AI 100-5e2025

    Establishes a global engagement plan to promote AI standards based on NIST risk management principles.

    • Monday, August 5th, 2024 (updated April, 2025)
    • Final
